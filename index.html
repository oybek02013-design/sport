🥊 MMA & UFC

Mixed Martial Arts - The Ultimate Combat Sport

🏆 What is MMA?

Mixed Martial Arts (MMA) is a full-contact combat sport that allows striking and grappling, both standing and on the ground, using a variety of techniques from different martial arts disciplines.

🔥 What is UFC?

UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) is the largest and most prestigious MMA promotion company in the world. Founded in 1993, UFC has revolutionized combat sports and brought MMA to global prominence.

📊 Key Facts

  • Weight Classes: 10 divisions from Flyweight to Heavyweight
  • Rules: Unified Rules of Mixed Martial Arts
  • Round System: 3 rounds (5 minutes each) for non-title, 5 rounds for championship
  • Governing Body: Athletic commissions regulate UFC events
  • Global Reach: Events held in over 30 countries worldwide
